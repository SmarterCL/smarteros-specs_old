## ğŸ”‘ Key Principles

1. **One OS, Many Countries**
   - Same core technology everywhere
   - Localized only at UI/content layer
   - Updates deploy globally

2. **One Store, One Checkout**
   - No fragmentation of product catalog
   - Unified customer accounts
   - Cross-border purchasing enabled

3. **MCP as Quality Standard**
   - Certification ensures compatibility
   - Partners benefit from trust
   - Customers avoid vendor lock-in

4. **Open Core, Paid Extensions**
   - SmarterOS core is open (GitHub)
   - Premium templates/apps are paid
   - Community contributions encouraged
